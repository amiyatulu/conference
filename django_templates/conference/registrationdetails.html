Abstract submission:

Abstract should contain only new and unpublished material and it must be submitted through on-line by the abstract deadline. Each abstract should not exceed 3000 characters including title, list of authors, affiliation and summary. You will receive an email prompt following successful completion of all fields. In each session, we will reserve time for at least one short talk selected from the submitted abstracts. Please indicate your presentation preference.

Please follow the bottom link for registration and submission of an abstract. If you encounter any problem, please write to us: jaganpongubala@gmail.com. A response will be sent you within 24 hrs.

Meeting Venue and Poster Area:

All meeting sessions will be held in the Auditorium of the School of Life Sciences, whereas the poster sessions will be held in the main Atrium of the School of Life Sciences.

On-Site housing:

Early registered participants will have an option to stay in a single or shared room in University of Hyderabad Guest on a priority basis.

Audio Visual Information:

The School of Life Sciences auditorium is recently constructed and it has a full range of audio visual equipment. LCD projector offers 1024X768 resolution and it can accommodate both PCs and MACs.

Presentation:

Speakers are anticipated to bring their presentation either in a flash drive or CD. Alternatively, speakers can use their laptop computer to connect to the LCD projector. The audio-visual system can be connected using a 15-pin VGA cable. Those who use laptop that requires proprietary cables should bring appropriate adaptors to establish the connectivity to the LCD projector.

WiFi:

The School of Life Sciences will provide WiFi connectivity to all participants during the conference.

Additional Information:

All questions related to registration, accommodation, abstract submission or any other queries should be directed to jaganpongubala@gmail.com
